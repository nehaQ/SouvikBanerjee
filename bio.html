Hey. Souvik here!

I'm Souvik Banerjee, a Kolkata-based photographer with a style based on observation rather than orchestration. I thrive off the emotions on show during an event, I take honest photographs with life and energy, I love photographing people interacting, capturing real and un-self conscious moments.

I won't interrupt you, ask you to pose unnaturally or ask you to do anything just for the sake of the camera. I'll ensure you stay relaxed and enjoy your day spending quality time with your family and friends - just as it should be. I'm looking for happiness in my images - your happiness, the happiness of your family and friends.

I cover social events, theatre and dance events, sports and, recently, portfolios. I co-founded a team called The Wedding Projector and cover weddings with them. So far I've covered 400+ events.

Away from photography I'm a pretty simple guy really - I enjoy sports, music, art and a bit of history and travel.

Thanks!
